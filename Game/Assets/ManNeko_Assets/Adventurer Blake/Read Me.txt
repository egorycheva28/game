Adventurer Blake

“Blake - the adventurer”. For those indie game designers who do not have a rigged model, 
i offer the great adventurer, Blake, the Nate’s cousin. With Extra set of animations.

This one is free for you!


-Humanoid Model -> you can use Unity Humanoid compatible animations with this character.

-PBR Textures -> In addition to normal map and albedo the character and gun have 
                textures that control metallic (R channel), smoothness(Alpha channel) 
                 and occlusion (GB channels).

-Blend Shapes -> gun has two Blend Shapes you can animate or control by code.
